Air-A is an asthma management app designed to help users better understand and manage their asthma through real-time environmental monitoring and symptom tracking.
Features

Overall Asthma Score – Provides a personalized asthma risk score based on current environmental conditions.

Environmental Risk Breakdown – Shows the specific factors affecting the score, including air quality, pollen, humidity, temperature, and more.

Detailed Insights – Highlights which environmental factors are contributing most to increased asthma risk.

Symptom Logging – Allows users to record symptoms and asthma episodes for ongoing monitoring.

High-Risk Notifications – Sends alerts when environmental conditions indicate elevated asthma risk.

Trend Analysis – Combines environmental data and symptom logs to help users identify patterns and potential triggers over time.

Apple Watch Integration – View asthma scores, environmental conditions, and key health metrics directly from your wrist.

Goal

Air-A empowers individuals with asthma to make informed decisions about their daily activities by providing actionable insights based on environmental conditions and personal health data.
